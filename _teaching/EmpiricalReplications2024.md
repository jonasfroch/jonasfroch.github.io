Fro the past two years, I have been teaching the project course *Reproduction Workshop Sports Economics* to undergraduate Economics students at the WiSo Faculty, University of Cologne. This course introduces students to sports economics, familiarizes them with empirical research designs, and provides the skills it needs to reproduce a selected paper from the field.
